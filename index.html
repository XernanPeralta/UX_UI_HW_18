A Little About Me


I’m a UI designer about to finish the UC Berkeley UI/UX bootcamp!

My expertise lies in taking complex design problems and molding them into simple and beautiful designs. I also enjoy the structure behind coding and strive to make easy to read and efficient code, whether it’s in HTML or CSS.

When I’m not pushing pixels or coding, you can find me watching everything on Netflix with my partner, and 3d modeling. Or playing World of Warcraft.



My Background


After graduating from The Academy of Art University in 3D Modeling for Video Game Design, I continued to work at the American Eagle Outfitters Flagship location. I started as a part-time stock associate, becoming the Operations leader during my time there. I’m currently striving to become a UI Designer to help build products that all users can enjoy.
